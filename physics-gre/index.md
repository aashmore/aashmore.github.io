## Study Plan

- Diagnostic: take one practice exam early under timed conditions.
- Review: identify the highest-yield topics from missed questions.
- Practice: work short sets regularly rather than saving everything for one long session.
- Final week: focus on timing, formulas, and common question patterns.

## Test-Taking Strategies

- Do quick passes first: answer questions you can solve cleanly, mark the rest, and return.
- Watch units and limiting cases; they can often eliminate wrong answers quickly.
- Keep an eye on pacing and avoid spending too long on any single problem.
- After practice exams, review mistakes by topic rather than only by problem number.
